Background

Hailing from what my friends affectionately call a ‘singing cult,’ my formative years were steeped in music and performing arts. As I entered my late teens, curiosity beckoned me toward the world of digital technologies.

Armed with a knack for calculus and a flair for photography, I discovered programming- an art form that blends creativity with methodical precision. It’s a canvas with rules, yet infinite possibilities. Now, as a trained Software Engineer, I’ve traded sheet music for syntax; My compositions resonate through elegant algorithms, harmonizing data structures and orchestrating efficient solutions. Whether it’s a symphony of APIs or a concerto in cloud architecture, I thrive on turning complexity into clarity.

Today, I stand as a bright, bubbly, resilient, and reliable individual. I'm ready to contribute my unique blend of creativity and technical prowess to exciting projects. The question is- are you? Welcome to my melodic journey through bytes and bits!
