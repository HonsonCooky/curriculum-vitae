Background, Education, Links

Background:

Born and raised in what friends have described as a 'singing cult', a majority of my youth revolved around musical and thespian endeavors. In my late teens, a series of curiosities led me into a digital technologies class. Equipped with an ability for calculus, and the creative capacity for photography, programming was an activity that enabled me to exploit my creative and methodical strengths; An art form with rules, yet no bounds. Infatuated with this new venture, my life slowly transitioned from practicing piano to programming Python.

Since then, I've finished College, completed four years at University, and found a job as a developer. Today, I'm programming for a living, and for fun in my free time; Happily blurring the lines between "work" and "life".
